How to Use the Inventory Manager

  • Enter the amount of each ore you own in the [ # ] column.
  • AV% shows how close you are to 1 Ambrosia Value (AV) for each ore.
  • AVs is the number of Ambrosia Values you have for that ore.
  • Your data is saved automatically in your browser.
  • Totals and stats are shown above for your entire inventory.
